Whenever an admin adds a new user, they will receive an email with login information.

Click on the Login To Your Account button. The main login page will open. Enter your details and click on the Login button.

You will be prompted to add an OTP code which is shared on your email.

Enter the OTP code and click on the Login button. If you havenâ€™t received the email, you can request another by clicking on the Click to resend button below the Login button.

Upon logging in successfully, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

Here, you will be able to see details of the project(s) assigned to you.

On your dashboard, you can view multiple statistics such as the total number of projects, requests, and custodians, along with the sizes of files uploaded over time, recent active requests, etc.
