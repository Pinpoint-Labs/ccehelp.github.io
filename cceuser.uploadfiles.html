Upload Files

To upload files to any project, you will first need to go to the Projects page, by clicking on the Projects option from the sidebar.

A similar screen will open, displaying all the projects assigned to you.

Click on the View button to view the details of the project.

Click on the Open CCE Client button to open the project in client view.

A similar screen will open:

You can drag and drop or upload files, and add notes like a custodian here. You can either drop files into the upload box or select any source to upload files from such as My Device, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.

Click on Add More to add more files to the upload, else click on the Upload File button. You will be prompted to add a note regarding your uploaded file(s).

Enter your note, save, and continue to start your upload. You will be able to see your uploaded files at the bottom of your screen:
