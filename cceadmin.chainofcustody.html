You can generate Chain of Custody for the following:

  1. Projects
  2. Requests
  3. Custodians
  4. Jobs/Files

You can select all or a few to be included in the Chain of Custody.

Projects

To generate the Chain of Custody of projects, open the Projects page and select the projects that need to be included in the log.

Click on Actions -> Chain of Custody.

A file preview will open up on your screen. Click on the Generate PDF button to generate the PDF:

Click on the Open PDF button to open the PDF in your browser:

Requests

To generate the Chain of Custody of requests, open a project’s detailed view and select the requests that need to be included in the log.

Click on Actions -> Chain of Custody.

A file preview will open up on your screen. Click on the Generate PDF button to generate the PDF:

Click on the Open PDF button to open the PDF in your browser:

Custodians
