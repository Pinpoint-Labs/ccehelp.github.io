CCE Admin

Log in

To log in to the portal, visit the following link: CrossCopy Portal

Enter your valid email address and password, and click on the Login button. You will receive an OTP code on your email address.

Enter the code in the portal screen and click on the Login button. If you haven’t received the email, you can request another by clicking on the Click to resend button below the Login button.

Upon successful login, your dashboard will open.

Reset Password

If you have forgotten your password or just want to reset it, follow these simple steps to reset your password and regain access to your account securely. On the main login screen, click on the button next to Forgot Password (below the Login button).

The following screen will open.

Enter your valid email address and click on Confirm. You will receive an email to reset your password.

Click on the Reset Your Password button. The following screen will appear:

Enter your new password and click on the Reset Password button. You will be redirected to the main login screen where you can log in with your email address and new password.

Set up a New Project

To create a new project, you will need to go to the Projects page. To access the Projects page, click on the Projects option from the navigation bar on the left-hand side.

You will see a similar screen.

Click on the Create New Project button in the top right corner to initiate a new project. A similar screen will appear.

Fill in all the required information, i.e.:

  1. Project Name: The name you want to give to your new project
  2. Project Description: Describe your project and what it aims to accomplish.
  3. Project Settings:
    • Upload Sources: Select the sources that your custodians can upload files from, such as selecting Local File will allow the custodian to upload files directly from their system, and Google Drive will allow the custodian to upload files via their Google Drive.
    • Target Source: Select the storage location you wish the files to be uploaded to.
    • AWS Bucket/Azure Container: Select or add information about your target source.
    • Enable File Verification: Select to enable verification of the files uploaded by custodians.

After you have added all the information, the Create Project button will become active. Click on the button to create the project.
If you wish to discard the project, click on the Cancel button to cancel creating the project. No information will be saved in this case.
After you have successfully created your project, it will appear in your Projects view with a status of Not Started indicating that it currently does not have any custodians and hence no activity performed on it.

For each project:

  1. Click on the View button to view the details of the project.
  2. Click on the gear icon to edit the project details.

Add Custodians to a Project

Custodians are the users who will upload files to the project. So for each project, you will have to add custodians who will have access to add files to your project.
To add custodians to a project, open the project details by clicking on the View button. Click on the Add Custodians button in the detail view.

You can add custodians one by one by filling in the information on the portal, or add in bulk by importing a CSV file.
To add custodians one by one, fill in the required fields:

Once you have filled in the details correctly, the Add Custodian button will become active. Click on it to add your custodian.

The newly added custodian will appear on the right side of the screen.

To delete a custodian, click on the red delete button () next to each custodian.
Once you have completed adding the custodians, click on the Finish button in the top right corner.

Import Bulk Custodians

If you wish to add more custodians, you can continue adding by either filling in the fields or by importing a CSV. To import from CSV, you will have to follow a template, which can be accessed by clicking on the information button -> Sample CSV File.

Once you have completed adding the custodians, click on the Finish button in the top right corner.

Monitoring Custodian Activities

To monitor a custodian’s activities, go to the custodian’s detailed view.

On this screen, you can view the following details of the custodian:

  1. Custodian’s name
  2. Custodian’s email address
  3. Custodian’s phone number

Moreover, you can see the Average Collection Time and Total Uploaded Size of the custodian, i.e. the average time it took the custodian to upload files, and the total size of all the files uploaded by the custodian, respectively.

You can also see the Total Files Uploaded by the custodian, along with how many of those were uploaded successfully and how many failed to upload.

The Jobs table showcases the jobs carried out by the custodian, i.e. files uploaded against the request.

The following information is displayed in the table:

  1. Number of files uploaded in each job
  2. The start time of each job
  3. Source of files for each job
  4. Status of each job
  5. Note added by the custodian on each job

You can select jobs to perform actions on them such as Create Verification Log, etc.

  1. Create File List
    Select the Create File List option to download a CSV containing the list and details of all the files in the selected jobs.
  2. Create Verification Log
    Select the Create Verification Log option to download a CSV containing the log of all the files in the selected jobs.
  3. Chain of Custody
    Select the Chain of Custody option to download a CSV containing the chain of custody of all the files in the selected jobs.

Click on the No. of Files for a specific job to open a detailed view of the job.

This view displays all the details of the selected job, including:

  1. Job’s ID
  2. Status of the job
  3. Start time in time and date format
  4. Note added by the custodian on the job
  5. The total size of all the files uploaded against the job

The Files table shows details of all the files uploaded against the job. You can apply filters on the table to view files by the uploaded source, such as Local System, Google Drive, etc. Moreover, you can add and remove columns from the table according to your needs.

Export Projects, Requests, Jobs, and Files

You can export your projects, requests, jobs, and files as CSV files via the respective export buttons.

Export Projects

To export projects, go to your Projects page and click on the Export All Projects button.

Export Requests

Open your desired project to view the project requests. You can export a few selected requests as well as all the requests generated.
To export a specific request, select the checkbox next to it - an Export Selected button will appear above the table. Click on the Export Selected button.

To export files of the custodians’ jobs, click on the respective custodian in a request. You will be able to see the details of the custodian and their jobs. Select all the jobs you wish to export the files of by clicking on the checkboxes next to them, then click on Actions -> Create File List.

Monitor Account Usage

To view your account’s usage, you will need to go to the Account Billing page. To access the Account Billing page, click on the Billing option from the navigation bar on the left-hand side.

You will see a similar screen.

On this page you will be able to see the following information:

  1. Company details
  2. Type of account, i.e. Demo or Commercial
  3. The expiry date of the account
  4. The number of files uploaded
  5. The total size of the files uploaded
  6. The number of custodians who have uploaded the files

In addition to viewing the information above, you can also edit your profile from this page. To do so, click on the Edit Profile button.

Request Increment of Usage Quota

If either your account’s expiry date passes, or you exceed any of your quotas (file count, uploaded size, collected custodians), you will start seeing a message on the top of your screen indicating that your license has either expired or reached its maximum limit.

To keep using your account, you will need to request an increment in your quota or your account’s expiration date. Go to your Billing page where you will see a message in red at the bottom.

Click on the Contact Administrator button. You will be prompted to provide information to the admin.

Select the restrictions you wish to renew - I have selected Collected Custodians and Expiry Date.

Enter your message to the admin with details such as increase custodians quota by 5, and then click on the Send Message button.
After successfully submitting the above form, you will be able to see a message on your Billing page, indicating that the message has been sent to the super admin.

After the admin has updated the restriction, the yellow message will no longer appear your custodians will be able to upload their files.
